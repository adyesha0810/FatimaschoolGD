Our Mission

Mission: Fatima Senior Secondary School wants to create a friendly and exciting place to learn. They want students to do well in school, be good people, and become leaders who help others.Our school aims to foster a love of learning in all students.

Our History

Fatima School also known as Fatima School. The school was established in 1976.Fatima School is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) . It is managed by The Catholic Educational Society.
